Smart Film

Filmbase Smart film is an electronic light control product made of liquid crystal/polymer hybrid material. It switches from opaque to transparent when an alternating current is applied. Enjoy seamless dimming control and experience privacy on demand as the smart film magically transforms glass surfaces, offering dynamic control at your fingertips.

Smart Film can be attached to glass surfaces to become Smart Glass.

High Temperature Smart Film

This versatile film, similar to laminated smart glass, offers dynamic light control by swiftly transitioning from opaque to transparent. With its impressive operating temperature range of -20°C to 105°C, it ensures reliable performance even in extreme conditions.

Smart Film for Cars

Smart Film for Cars is exclusively developed for automotive applications. This switchable smart film, made with a special substrate and wide temperature liquid crystal material, offers advanced functionality and performance.

More

PNLC Smart Film

PNLC Smart Film is also known as Adverse PDLC Film. Unlike regular PDLC smart film, PNLC film operates in reverse: it turns opaque when powered on and clear when powered off. Its versatile nature makes it ideal for both residential and commercial settings, providing privacy and dynamic control over the transparency of glass surfaces.

More

Smart Glass

Smart Glass, compared to Smart Film, is particularly suitable for spaces that require larger glass surfaces, such as architectural windows, glass facades, and skylights. This innovative sandwich structure combines PDLC film between two glass layers, allowing instant switching between opaque and transparent states.
