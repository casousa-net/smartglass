Filmbase's Transparent LED Screen Receives Excellence Award at the 10th China LED Pioneer Conference

2023/6/13 17:17:14 | 789

On June 9, 2023, the 10th "China LED Pioneer Award Ceremony” with the theme of "To start a golden decade of high-quality LED development in China" was held at the Langham Place Hotel in Guangzhou.

The "China LED Pioneer Conference" is the most influential event in China, known as the vane of global technology and market development. The forum is guided by the China Illuminating Society, held by the Semi-Special Committee of the China Illuminating Society and the Shenzhen Lighting and Display Engineering Industry Association. The "China LED Pioneer Award" award ceremony was also held at the same time as the forum, in recognition of the past year, the contribution of influential enterprises and innovative technology products.

This year's "China LED Pioneer Award" has a total of 278 companies participating in the selection. After China LED Pioneer Award's comprehensive evaluation of intellectual property innovation capabilities, Filmbase's "Transparent LED Screen" won the "10th China LED Pioneer Award" Excellence Award.

Transparent LED Screen of Filmbase  is an extraordinary innovation that has garnered significant attention and acclaim in the LED industry. This remarkable display technology has revolutionized the way we perceive and interact with digital signage, offering a unique and captivating visual experience. With its transparent properties and high image quality, Filmbase's LED Glass has emerged as a game-changer in the field of display solutions.

Filmbase's Transparent LED Screen boasts exceptional transparency, allowing viewers to see through the display while still delivering vibrant and dynamic content. This feature makes it an ideal choice for applications where preserving visibility is crucial, such as retail storefronts, museums, airports, exhibition halls, curtain walls and architectural media. The transparent nature of the screen seamlessly integrates with the surrounding environment, providing a visually stunning and immersive experience.

The 10th "China LED Pioneer Conference" not only provided an excellent industry-university-research communication platform for peers, but also promoted industrial innovation, upgrading and transformation, and created more opportunities for industrial cooperation. It is hoped that Filmbase will win more awards in the next conference, continue to give full play to its strong advantages in intellectual property innovation capabilities, re-accumulate momentum for the development of the LED industry, and promote the rapid and high-quality development of the global LED display industry.
