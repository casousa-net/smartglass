Filmbase invites you to AIA 2023 Conference in San Francisco, June, 2023

2023/4/19 14:58:52 | 1246

Filmbase, the leading provider of innovative film and glass solutions, is set to showcase our latest products at the AIA 2023 Conference on Architecture, as known as A’23 Con. The event is scheduled to take place on June 8-10, 2023, at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, CA.

Visitors to the conference will have the opportunity to explore Filmbase's cutting-edge products at Booth 4329, which is hosted by our exclusive distributor, Smart-Film-Blinds. We will be displaying our range of products including Color Smart Film, UTD, LED Glass, and Smart Film Blind.

Colored Smart Film, also known as Colored PDLC Film, is a smart film solution with various color options that allow users to decorate any glass surfaces. Main features of color smart film is pleased to look at while enhancing privacy and blocking UV rays.

UTD, or Ultrathin Transparency Display, is another groundbreaking technology from Filmbase that offers a transparent display on a thin film attached to glass surfaces. The foundation of an ultrathin transparency display is projector reflection imaging. It can be used to create immersive and interactive experiences for users.

LED Glass is a revolutionary new product that combines the functionality of glass with the beauty of LED lighting. The glass panels can be used to create stunning lighting effects in a range of settings, from tourist attractions to glass curtain walls.

Smart Film Blind is a versatile product that can be used to create in-window blinds for privacy's concerns. The film can be controlled to achieve different levels of transparency, opening animation, and support for non-traditional window shapes.

All Filmbase products mentioned above are shown in our YouTube channel and they have been widely recognized for their innovation and quality. The attendance of Filmbase at the A‘23 Conference is a testament to our commitment to providing architects and designers with the best tools they need, in order to create advanced buildings and spaces.

A’23 Con is expected to attract thousands of attendees from around the world, and Filmbase is looking forward to showcasing our latest products to this diverse audience. Visitors to Booth 4329 will have the opportunity to speak with Filmbase representatives and learn more about our inventive film and glass solutions. Be sure you sign up for the Architecture Expo before visiting us on June 8 and June 9.
