​Filmbase Announces Strategic Partnership with India's KK Holding

2023/7/27 14:39:32 | 601

Filmbase, the leading innovator in smart film technology, has recently signed a strategic partnership agreement with the renowned Indian conglomerate, KK Holding. The signing ceremony took place at Filmbase headquarters on 25th July, and was attended by key representatives from both companies.

From KK Holding, the delegation included the esteemed CEO, Mr. Kapil Shukla, and the Vice President, Mr. Shivashankar Madhukar. Established in 2013, KK Holding boasts a diverse portfolio spanning various industries. With over two decades of collective experience, the company has excelled in commercial facade construction, hospitality, real estate, education & social sector, packed food, and hotel businesses. As a conglomerate with more than a decade of successful operations, KK Holding has nurtured a strong network of expertise in multiple sectors, making it a highly respected player in the Indian business landscape.

The collaboration between Filmbase and KK Holding marks a significant milestone in the smart film industry. Filmbase's cutting-edge Smart Film technology offers a wide range of applications, perfectly aligning with KK Holding's main businesses:

Commercial Facade Construction: Filmbase Smart Film enhances energy-efficient windows in buildings, dynamically adjusting tint to control sunlight and heat.

Hospitality: Smart Film provides privacy solutions in hotel rooms and interactive displays in lobbies, enhancing guest experiences.

Real Estate: Smart windows attract potential buyers with a modern appearance and contribute to energy savings in buildings.

Education & Social Sector: Interactive displays create engaging learning environments, and privacy solutions ensure confidentiality in meeting rooms.

Hotel Businesses: Smart Film applications in guest rooms, conference rooms, and common areas elevate guest experiences with customizable privacy and projection screens.

The well-received applications of Filmbase Smart Film in various sectors demonstrate its potential to revolutionize glass surface usage. Under the terms of the strategic partnership, Filmbase and KK Holding will leverage their complementary strengths to expand the reach of Filmbase Smart Film in the Indian market. KK Holding's extensive experience and established presence across diverse industries will provide Filmbase with valuable insights and access to new opportunities.
