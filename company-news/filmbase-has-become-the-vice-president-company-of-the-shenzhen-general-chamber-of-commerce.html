FILMBASE has become the Vice President company of the Shenzhen General Chamber of Commerce

2023/10/13 16:52:40 | 179

In October 2023, FILMBASE became the Vice President company of the Shenzhen General Chamber of Commerce (SZGCC). Established in 2005, SZGCC is a highly influential chamber of commerce in China, committed to empowering Shenzhen businesses and building an international chamber of commerce. SZGCC adheres to laws, regulations, and social ethics, serving its members and contributing to Shenzhen's rapid economic growth.

FILMBASE's appointment as the Vice President Company at SGZCC holds immense importance. It not only validates our company's reputation but also enhances our influence in the industry. This recognition allows us to actively participate in shaping industry strategies, exploring market trends, and seizing new opportunities. Engaging with business leaders provides us with a platform to share our insights widely, fostering enhanced partnerships.

This honor motivates FILMBASE to redouble efforts in delivering outstanding services, creating developmental opportunities for our employees, and strengthening our industry impact. We are excited about the growth prospects and collaborations that this achievement brings, solidifying FILMBASE's position as a leader in the business community.

In the future, with the support of SZGCC, FILMBASE will fulfill its duties as the Vice President, enhance its competitiveness, achieve rapid business development, and promote its products globally.
