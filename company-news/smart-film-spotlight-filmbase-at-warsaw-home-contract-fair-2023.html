Smart Film Spotlight: FILMBASE at Warsaw Home & Contract Fair 2023

2023/11/10 14:42:10 | 89

FILMBASE was invited to join the Warsaw Home & Contract Interior Design Contract Fair and showcased our full range of Smart Film Products, including Smart Film, Color Smart Film, PNLC Smart Film, Etch Film, and more. Among them, Transparent LED Glass attracted the most attention, impressing visitors with advanced technology and modern design.

This event not only highlighted FILMBASE's commitment to creative Smart Glass design solutions but also established FILMBASE as a standout in the industry, capturing the attention of both professionals and enthusiasts.
