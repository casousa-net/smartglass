2023 Filmbase Summer Party event

2023/7/4 14:13:34 | 733

On June 30, 2023, Filmbase hosted a summer party themed "Together, Create and Win" at the Shenzhen Golden Bay Beach Resort. This event served as a retrospective of the significant breakthroughs and growth achieved by Filmbase in the first half of the year, while also outlining plans and prospects for accelerated development in the second half, aiming to strengthen team cohesion and expand the brand's influence.

The event commenced with speeches from Filmbase executives, expressing gratitude to all employees and acknowledging their hard work. Mr. Zhu Longshan, Co-Founder, emphasized the importance of the team's beliefs and spirit of hard work in achieving new milestones at Filmbase. He encouraged employees to continue learning and growing while dedicating themselves to Filmbase.

During his speech, Mr. Zhu reviewed Filmbase's tremendous breakthroughs and growth in the first half of the year. In particular, he highlighted the invention of UTD and the upgrade of LED glass screens, signifying significant progress in technological innovation and product quality at Filmbase. He also praised the performance of the domestic and international sales departments and expressed gratitude for the collective efforts and support of all employees. Additionally, Mr. Zhu outlined the company's future development direction and shared important information regarding the market, brand, and production.

Following Mr. Zhu's address, Domestic Sales President Huang and Co-Founder Yuan summarized the achievements of the first half of the year and motivated everyone to continue their efforts towards mutual success in the second half. While reviewing the accomplishments of the first half, Domestic Sales President Huang and Co-Founder Yuan commended the hard work and professional competence demonstrated by all employees. They highlighted Filmbase's significant achievements in market expansion, product innovation, and customer satisfaction, expressing sincere gratitude for the employees' dedication. Subsequently, department leaders led their teams on stage to showcase their team slogans, demonstrating team cohesion and confidence in achieving the goals for the second half of the year.

After the speeches, all employees formed a circle to participate in the interactive activity "Crab Dance". Following a group photo, Filmbase employees enjoyed free time taking photos, participating in KTV entertainment, or strolling on the beach. During dinner, everyone had a buffet and engaged in activities such as KTV, bonfire parties, and whole lamb barbecue. The event venue was specially arranged to showcase Filmbase's core products, LED Glass and UTD, vividly demonstrating the company's innovative technology and excellent quality.

Filmbase has always prioritized employee welfare and placed their happiness at the core of the company's development. During this event, Filmbase once again conveyed care and support to the employees, providing them with a moment to relax and enjoy themselves. Moving forward, Filmbase will continue to organize a variety of colorful activities to bring welfare to employees.

Through this summer party event, Filmbase once again showcased its unity and positive spirit as a highly cohesive group. We believe that with the collective efforts of all employees, Filmbase will continue to achieve greater breakthroughs and success in the second half of the year.
