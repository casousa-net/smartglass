FILMBASE was Invited to Participate in the "China-ASEAN Emerging Industries Partnering Conference" Showcasing Outstanding Innovations

2023/9/26 15:44:20 | 336

On the afternoon of September 21st, 2023, the "China (Shenzhen, Hong Kong) - ASEAN (Kuala Lumpur) Emerging Industries Partnering Conference" took place in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. As a national high-tech enterprise and one of the pioneering enterprises representing Shenzhen and Guangdong Province, FILMBASE was invited to participate in this partnering conference. The company showcased innovative products and technological achievements while sharing unique insights into industrial development.

The partnering conference was hosted by the Shenzhen Municipal People's Government, Commerce and Economic Development Bureau of Hong Kong, and the Hong Kong Trade Development Council (HKTDC). It was organized by the Industrial and Information Technology Bureau of Shenzhen, Shenzhen Municipal Commerce Bureau, Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office of the People's Government of Shenzhen Municipality, and  Service Bureau for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises of Shenzhen Municipality. 

The event aimed to boost cooperation and exchanges in emerging industries among Shenzhen, Hong Kong, and ASEAN countries, facilitating investment cooperation and providing a platform for industrial collaboration. Over 200 participants, including industry leaders, associations, experts, and media from the region, attended the conference. Co-Founder of FILMBASE,  Mr. Zhu Longshan and Ms. Yuan Lanlan were both present at the event.

During the conference, FILMBASE showcased representative products, Transparent LED Display and Smart Film, which received great attention from government officials and businesses. Many Malaysian companies expressed interest in becoming distributors for FILMBASE's innovative and competitive products in Malaysia.

Qin Weizhong, Mayor of the Shenzhen Municipal Government and representatives from the Malaysian government inspecting FILMBASE’s products.

FILMBASE is a globally renowned supplier of Smart Film, Smart Glass and Transparent LED Display glass. Our products and services are available in over 70 countries and regions worldwide, collaborating with more than 10,000 global processors and 30,000 architectural firms and professionals.

Co-Founder Mr. Zhu Longshan has dedicated over a decade to pioneering new materials, utilizing his innovative capabilities and extensive experience to collaborate with technical experts from around the world. Together, they have conducted research and development to break away from traditional technical formulas and production processes. In 2017, a series of products were officially introduced to the market, receiving unanimous acclaim from customers both domestically and internationally.

FILMBASE is committed to exploring advanced new material solutions, embracing the principles of green, environmentally friendly, and sustainable development, with a vision to provide people with a safe, comfortable, and intelligent way of life.
