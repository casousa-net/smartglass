Futuristic Innovation: Filmbase Presents Transparent LED Screen at Shenzhen Industrial Museum

2023/8/15 15:58:58 | 612

On August 14, 2023,  Filmbase was honored to be invited by the Shenzhen government to participate in the Development Countries Industrial Exchange Conference and presented the LED Glass product. The event, held on July 27th at the Shenzhen Industrial Museum, aimed to foster international collaboration and promote outstanding products.

At the conference, government officials from nations including the African Union and Peru had the opportunity to explore the remarkable products showcased at the 24th China High-Tech Fair. Shenzhen Industrial Museum provided in-depth insights into industrial development, exceptional enterprises, and their relevant products. Through proactive coordination and engagement, participating enterprises were able to establish meaningful connections with overseas markets, particularly with the African Union and Peru, expanding their global presence.

Stepping into the exhibition hall, a captivating glass screen instantly captured visitors' attention. This unique installation marked Filmbase's presence at the Shenzhen Industrial Museum, showcasing the signature product - Filmbase Transparent LED Screen. This revolutionary display technology offers unparalleled versatility and an array of applications.

The Transparent LED Display is constructed from RGB magic lamp beads that can be seamlessly combined to create displays of any size. Its innovative design renders it suitable for various applications, including glass curtain walls, advertising media, interior design, commercial displays, and functional signage. The possibilities are limited only by one's imagination.

Noteworthy features of the Filmbase LED Glass include its exceptional daylight visibility, guaranteeing clear video playback even under bright sunlight. With multiple LED bead pitch options available, such as P0505, P0510, P1010, P1020, and P20*20, users can achieve precise and detailed displays. The option to customize width and transparency further enhances its versatility.

Incorporating high-performance materials, the Glass LED Screen boasts a screen made from waterproof, anti-corrosion, and anti-impact tempered glass, ensuring durability and enduring performance. Additionally, the exclusive patented screen repair technology simplifies the replacement of faulty lamp beads, streamlining maintenance procedures and minimizing downtime.

Filmbase's presence at the Shenzhen Industrial Museum underscores their commitment to innovation and advancement in the realm of display technology. By showcasing the LED Glass Display, Filmbase continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of visual communication, leaving an indelible mark on the landscape of industrial exhibitions and technological innovation.
