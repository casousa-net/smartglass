  • Silent Pod

    The Silent Pod from Filmbase is the perfect solution for creating quiet and private spaces within office environments. These booths are ideal for conducting confidential meetings, phone calls, or focused work that requires minimal distractions. Whether placed in open-plan offices or shared workspaces, the Silent Pod provides a secluded area where employees can concentrate and collaborate effectively.

  • Transparent LED Screen

    The Transparent LED Screen offered by Filmbase is a groundbreaking display solution for office spaces. This innovative technology combines transparency and dynamic visual content, enabling businesses to showcase information, advertisements, or interactive presentations while maintaining visibility through the screen. Transparent LED Screens are ideal for reception areas, conference rooms, or exhibition spaces, where businesses can engage visitors and leave a lasting impression.

  • Smart Film

    Filmbase's Smart Film technology revolutionizes privacy and flexibility in office spaces. This innovative product allows you to control the transparency of glass partitions, windows, and doors at the touch of a button. With Smart Film, you can transform transparent surfaces into opaque ones, creating instant privacy for meeting rooms, executive offices, or collaborative areas. This adaptable solution enables you to create dynamic spaces that cater to changing needs and enhance productivity.

    Find out more about Smart Film

  • Smart Curtain

    Filmbase's Smart Curtain is designed to provide customizable control over sunlight in office spaces. The main function of the Smart Curtain is to regulate the amount of sunlight entering the room. By raising the curtain, you can enjoy natural sunlight in the morning, while lowering it to reduce glare and avoid excessive brightness. This feature helps create a comfortable and well-lit workspace, allowing employees to adjust the lighting conditions to their preference. The Smart Curtain adds convenience and energy efficiency by utilizing natural light while minimizing glare and heat.

    Find out more about Smart Curtain
