Application of smart glass in green building

2023/6/12 11:57:36 | 613

Smart  glass is a kind of glass that can adjust the intensity of light by changing its appearance. It has the application potential of green buildings such as energy saving, environmental protection and comfort.


Switchable glass can achieve these functions by coating the glass surface with different colored coatings or film layers. These coatings or layers can be transparent or opaque, giving the glass varying degrees of clarity and light intensity.


The following are some applications of dimming glass in green buildings:


Solar control: 

Smart  glass can control the transmittance of light by coating different colors of coatings or films, so as to make buildings brighter or darker and reduce energy consumption.


For example, it can be coated with color-forming or multifunctional layers to control transmission and reduce temperature.

For example, it can be transparent or opaque, reducing energy consumption.


In conclusion, dimming glass has broad application prospects in green buildings and can be used for multiple purposes, such as solar control, thermal insulation, interior decoration, etc. With the continuous development of technology and the continuous reduction of cost, it is believed that switchable glass will have more applications and developments in green buildings in the future.
